About Me

I fell in love with web development quite unexpectedly. Disillusioned with the world of education, by recommendation of a family member, I took an online class in making websites. What I didn't expect was to enjoy and be good at building websites. Intellectually, coding combines the problem solving and cool head of rock climbing with the precision of quilting.

JavaScript
